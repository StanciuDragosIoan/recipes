Chocolate balls

Easy to make chocolate bars, delicious and healthy!

Ingredients:

600ml of milk (270cal 18P )

170g sugar (680 cal )

15g butter (90 cal )

180g cocoa powder (410 cal )

Total: 20 pieces = 1450 cal (70 cal/ piece)

Vanilla essence

Steps:

Put milk in a skillet

Add sugar

Stir and melt the sugar (boil over medium heat)

When sugar is melted add the butter

Add 5-6 drops of vanilla essence

Mix well until it starts to boil (medium heat still)

When it starts to boil, alternate between medium/low heat so that it won't overflow

Keep this up until it becomes yellow and thick

Turn off the gas

Pour condensed milk in a bowl

Add 100g of sieved cocoa powder

Mix well

When homogeneous, add 30g more cocoa poweder

Mix again

Put everything on a flat plate with plastic foil on it

Flatten it and wrap it in foil (put in freezer for 40 min)

When composition is cold, set it on a plate (without plastic foil) onto which you have sieved 1 more layer of cocoa powder

Sprinkle more cocoa powder on top of it and cut it into small squares

Form the squares as balls in your hand or as actual squares

Set nicely on plate
